Fundraising

Youth for Palestinian Freedom typically aims to support lasting change rather than damage control. However, given the severity of the situation in Gaza and the tremendous extent of the damages caused, all money will go directly to Gaza.

Our Mission

We aim to fundraise money in creative ways in order to support political lobbying efforts. This way we can call for real sustainable peace and change. Rather than helping limit the damage done to families as a result of Israeli attacks we want to cut off the problems at the root cause. That root is the United States' financial and military support for Israel.

Youth for Palestinian Freedom

Dedicated to advocating for Palestine through community action, fundraising, and public awareness. Together, we can make a difference!
