Boycotting

Boycotting is a powerful form of peaceful protest that allows individuals and organizations to take a stand against injustice. By refusing to support companies or entities that perpetuate oppression or discrimination, we send a clear message: ethical practices matter. Boycotting can drive significant social and political change by applying economic pressure to institutions.
