Youth for Palestinian Freedom

Empowering the youth of America to Boycott, protest, and educate. Our mission is to ignite a collective movement among young people to stand in solidarity with oppressed communities worldwide. By fostering awareness, encouraging active participation, and creating spaces for learning, we aim to inspire the next generation to challenge injustices, advocate for freedom, and support human rights. Through educational campaigns, grassroots activism, and partnerships with like-minded organizations, we are committed to creating lasting change and shaping a future driven by justice, equality, and peace
